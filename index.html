About me

Hey, my name is Lova a.k.a Aresthegreek, I’m a Graphic designer who gravitates toward clean concepts, bold structure, and visuals that stand on their own. My work is shaped by influences I naturally connect with: Greek culture, with its sense of myth, symbolism, and timeless geometry. Brutalism, for its raw honesty and architectural strength, and grunge, for its texture, grit, and unapologetic character. 

I focus on crafting designs that are sharp, intentional, and built around a clear identity whether it’s a logo, cover art, or a complete visual direction. My process is simple and grounded: understand the idea, refine it with purpose, and create something that communicates instantly and confidently. 

I enjoy exploring new techniques and pushing my aesthetic without overcomplicating things. For me, design isn’t about trying to impress, it's about creating visuals that feel solid, memorable, and true to the vision.
